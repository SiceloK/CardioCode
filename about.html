About

Cardio.com is a health and wellness online fitness centre. We offer a new online experience for those who value a family-friendly and attention-to-detail approach. Thanks to our wide array of personal trainers and fitness studio experts, customized inspiration and personalized results are waiting just for you!

The mission of Cardio.com is to be more than “just an online gym.” We know that too many local gym memberships go unused and guilt builds up. Cardio.com is here to support you, every step of the way. On our online fitness studio ensures that you exercise in the comfort of your home, park or place where you can go online.
Whether you’re working with a personal trainer for one-on-one time or logging in for one of our many yoga classes, we’ll be happy to see you not matter what and know you’ll feel the same. We’re interested in helping as many people who have busy schedules to make time for a physical visit to the gym as healthy as possible.
